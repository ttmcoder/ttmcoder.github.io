When I was little, my older brother and I would make little home movies and skits with each other for fun. We would make them under the name of our "studio" we came up with, IF STUDIOS.

I've always had a love and passion for artistic photography and making an image look the best it could be. Capturing the scenery of my home state, North Carolina, especially during the season of autumn was always a fun treat.

Our goal here at IF STUDIOS is to provide the best quality photographs we can, be they of a scenic location, portraits of families, or capturing parties and events for you to remember.
